Dr. Shubhnandan Singh Jamwal

Associate Professor, Computer Science and IT

Working as faculty members at University of Jammu Since 2006 and has more than twenty years of teaching and research experience at post graduate level and UG level. Published four books as a single author in programming languages, and the title of the books are Programming in C ISBN: 978-93-325-2561-0, JAVA-9 ISBN: 978-93-5213-776-3, Executing C: a Practical Approach ISBN: 81-89478-14-1 & Garbage Collectors on Modern Compilers ISBN: 978-3-8443-1213-3.

Major Research Project entitled Development of Shallow parser Tool for 12 Indian Languages (SPT-IL) funded by Ministry of Communication and Information Technology, Government of India, has also been completed.

My Research interests include Natural Language Processing (Specially on Dogri Language Data Sets), Speech Processing, Machine Learning & Programming Paradigms. The Academic Interests are teaching programming languages like Java, C++, C, Python & Web Technologies.
